A small personal collection of scarves that have been collected over the course of 3 years. They vary in size, material, texture, color(s), and pattern(s) giving them all their own unique look and style. There are 3 scarves listed for each category, and my collection will continue to build even more over time. I have a love for scarves, because they are add more excitement to an outfit, and help to keep your neck warm during the colder weather. Each one posted here has their own story on how they were acquired, and they all come from many different locations.

Decorative

The decorative scarves all vary in wonderful designs and materials giving them strong imagery, and very soft textures. These all come from many friends, and thrift store adventures.

Solids

The “simplistic” scarves are all made out of different material, and created in a way that is mainly based off solid patterns.
